The Problem

Modern infrastructure systems operate across deeply interconnected compute, orbital, networking, and operational layers. Most organizations lack deterministic visibility into how failures propagate across dependencies and escalate into large-scale outages.

The Sextant Solution

Sextant models infrastructure relationships as deterministic dependency graphs, enabling engineering teams to simulate node degradation, cascade propagation, systemic destabilization, and resilience recovery pathways before deployment.
