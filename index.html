The Problem

Modern infrastructure systems are deeply interconnected across orbital, cloud, networking, compute, and operational layers. Most organizations lack deterministic visibility into how failures propagate across dependencies and escalate into large-scale outages.

The Sextant Solution

Sextant models infrastructure dependencies as deterministic graph structures, enabling engineering teams to simulate node degradation, cascade propagation, systemic destabilization, and resilience recovery outcomes before deployment.
