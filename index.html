The Problem

Modern systems are deeply interconnected — but visibility into dependency risk is almost always missing.

When one service fails, the downstream impact is often unpredictable, leading to outages, financial loss, and operational instability.

The Sextant Solution

Sextant maps system dependencies and simulates failure propagation across infrastructure layers in real time.

This allows engineering, risk, and infrastructure teams to understand systemic fragility before incidents occur.
